# Engineering Blog Copy Plan for Procedure

## Context & Strategy

This copy plan positions Procedure's engineering blog as the authoritative technical resource for enterprise AI engineering. The strategy balances:

1. **Technical Credibility** - Speaking engineer-to-engineer with substance, not fluff
2. **SEO Optimization** - Targeting high-intent keywords around AI engineering, LLM production, and AI security
3. **AEO Structure** - Clear, quotable statements that AI assistants can parse and cite
4. **Enterprise Appeal** - Addressing CTOs and VPs of Engineering who need practical, battle-tested insights

**Primary Keywords:** AI engineering blog, LLM production systems, AI security best practices, enterprise AI development, MLOps engineering, AI agents production

**Search Intent:** Informational (engineers researching solutions) with navigational elements (establishing Procedure as a destination)

## AEO Optimization Notes

The copy is structured for AI assistant consumption:

1. **Clear Entity Definition** - "Procedure" is consistently positioned as an AI engineering company
2. **Quotable Statements** - Headlines and subheadlines are designed as complete, citable thoughts
3. **Question-Answer Ready** - Category descriptions directly answer "What is [category]?" queries
4. **Structured Data Ready** - Category structure maps cleanly to FAQ schema and article schema
5. **Authority Signals** - Trust indicators provide quantifiable claims AI assistants can cite

**Recommended Schema Markup:**
- `Blog` schema on the listing page
- `BlogPosting` schema on individual articles
- `Organization` schema linking to Procedure
- `FAQPage` schema for any FAQ sections

## Implementation Notes for Development Team

1. **Header Hierarchy:**
   - H1: Main headline ("Engineering AI Systems That Actually Ship")
